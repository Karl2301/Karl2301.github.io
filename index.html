About Us

Our association aims to protect wild animals while respecting their way of life.

Our commitment to wildlife conservation in Kenya.


As a member of our organization, you will be joining a dedicated team working towards wildlife conservation in Kenya. We firmly believe that preserving biodiversity is crucial for ensuring a sustainable future for all inhabitants of the planet, including wild species. We conduct awareness campaigns, on-ground conservation projects and work closely with local communities to protect endangered species and their natural habitats. As a member of our association, you will be involved in exciting initiatives and be part of the fight to protect the diversity of nature and preserve our heritage for future generations. Join us in our mission to protect wildlife and safeguard biodiversity for a sustainable future.
